Project Overview & Key Features

1. Executive Summary

Project Overview

A real-time market screener that continuously monitors financial markets, generates alerts based on technical indicators, and provides automated trading decision support with multi-channel notifications.

Purpose

Automate market scanning and trading opportunity identification to save time and improve decision-making efficiency for traders.

Key Features

Real-time Processing

Market data processing at 1-minute intervals during US market hours (9:30 AM - 4:00 PM EST, Mon-Fri)

Multiple Alert Types

MACD cycles, Price Action, EMA touches, High/Low breaks, Trend Continuation with customizable thresholds

Automated Decisions

Decision engine with buy/sell signals and position management using EMA-based selling strategies

Multi-channel Notifications

In-app (SSE), Push notifications, Email alerts with user preference management

Market Hours Detection

Intelligent US market hours detection with automatic scanning activation/deactivation

Position Management

EMA-based selling with profit optimization and comprehensive trade tracking

System Capabilities

Advanced market monitoring and automated trading decision support for internal team use. The system is designed for automated market monitoring and can be extended with custom features as needed.
